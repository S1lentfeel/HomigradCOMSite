Homigrad Community Rules

General Provisions

1. On interpreting the rules:

Do not try to look for loopholes, vulnerabilities, or other tricks to claim, as if the rule does not cover a particular situation or cannot be applied. We are all human, and are well aware of the nature of such manipulations.

2. Scope:

The rules apply to interactions within the Homigrad community (e.g., Garry's Mod server, Discord, VK, and other platforms where moderated activity is possible) and may apply outside of it in cases involving threats, identity leaks, and similar situations.

3. Mandatory familiarization:

These rules are mandatory for all community members to familiarize themselves with. Ignorance of the rules does not absolve from responsibility.

4. Punishment for violations of the rules:

Punishments are always strict. In-game (Garry's Mod) ban from 1 day to 8 weeks. In the community (Discord) mut from a day to a week, or blocking.

Rules of behavior in the game (Garry's Mod Homigrad Servers)

1. Use of bugs:

Using game bugs and flaws, as well as hiding information about them.

Getting into areas that are inaccessible in normal game play (such as behind the map or on invisible surfaces).

Using methods that give an unfair advantage (dupes, abuses, immortality, etc.).

2. Intentional non-game killing:

Purposefully and systematically harming a player for personal motives unrelated to gameplay (killing, wounding, etc.).

Applies only if a complaint is filed.

2. RDM (Random Deathmatch):

It is forbidden to attack players without a game-related reason.

Mass attacks without distinction of purpose are considered RDM, and targeting a single player is considered bias.

2. Monitoring:

It is forbidden to pass information to living players while dead.

If you are revived after death, it is allowed to use information obtained before revival.

General rules

1. Discrediting the project:

Discrediting the project, spreading false information.

Advertising of third-party projects.

NSFW content, swastikas or other offensive content (according to Garry's Mod rules).

Threats or leaks of members' personal data.

Distributing malware.

Use or distribution of cheats, as well as modifications to the game client.

For violation of this rule blocking on all our resources - forever

2. Unacceptable Behavior (Bullying):

Bullying, harassment, insults.

Provocation, ethnic animosity or humiliation of a person for his principles.

If the actions are not directed at a specific person or are of a playful nature, the administration has the right to leave them unaddressed, except in cases of repeated violations.

Violation occurs if both parties disagree with these insults, or the person can not file a complaint (weak-willed, few years old)

For violation of this rule blocking on all our resources - from a week or forever.

3. It is forbidden to impersonate the administration:

Selling fake services.

Conducting checks on behalf of the administration.

For violation of this rule blocking on all our resources - permanently

Procedure for handling violations

1. General Principles:

Violations are reviewed by the administration, which determines the punishment.

The higher administration has the right to review the decision of the lower (purchased) administrators and make changes (e.g. remove the ban or penalize the administrator).

2. Filing Complaints:

Complaints are filed via [Discord server]

The main proof is a video recording. Filing false complaints is penalized.

3. Handling complaints against the administration:

Complaints against the administration are handled by the curator or super-admins

In case of disagreement with the decision of the administration, you can appeal to a higher authority.

4. Unbanning:

The decision to unban is made by the administrator who issued the punishment or by a higher administration.

In the case of an agreement on unbanning with donat the transaction is carried out officially. Sale of unbans or other services on behalf of the administration is strictly prohibited.

Regulations on the exercise of rights

1. It is forbidden to use rights for the purpose of:

Gaining an advantage in the game.

Disruption of the server. (Discrediting the project)

Humiliating, bullying or insulting other members. (Unacceptable Behavior (Bullying))

Violation of the above 2 rules will be punished with the highest penalties.

2. Organization of Events:

СThe creation of global events is only allowed with the consent of the majority of players (more than 70% or better 80%).

Global events are those events that change the gameplay.

3. Relationships:

Relationships between admins should be neutral and professional.

Notes

The activity of recruiting administrators is mandatory. In case of ignoring duties, warnings or revocation of privileges are possible.

All admins are obliged to moderate the server (if they are in place (discord, garry's mod)), in case of ignoring violations you will be penalized.

All actions of members and administrators are governed by this document. Please read it carefully to avoid violations.

When blocking an account, the reason must quote the rule (Project Discretion) or its clause (NSFW)
